KOSMOS

Kings League Infojobs | Queens League Oysho

Motion design

The Kings League Infojobs is a seven-a-side football league in Barcelona established in 2022 by Gerard Piqué, in association with Ibai Llanos and other notable football personalities and internet streamers.

I played a role in creating visually engaging content that enhanced the overall spectator experience. My primary focus was on developing dynamic motion graphics and animations specifically tailored for the LED screens present at the stadiums.These animations are a few examples of the LED screens served as a crucial component of the live events,providing real-time information, enhancing the atmosphere, and adding an extra layer of excitement to the matches and shows.
